I am just trying the game out

Welcome, when playing the demo mode, you will be automatically given a secret phrase of 12 words. In order to find the secret phrase, you will need to collect the 12 items in the order that is displayed in the Pathfind tool. Once you are able to control your character, you can interact with the Pathfind tool by pressing 'P' or using the button on the top-left corner.
You can click each button to get directions of where the item is and once you find it, you can collect it by pressing 'E'. Make sure to select the color of the item as displayed in the Pathfind tool. Once you have collected all 12 items, a small key button will appear to the bottom of the screen. Click that, and select Extract to find your secret phrase! You can opt to fill out the first Google Form, if you haven't done already. Thank you for trying the game.
If you would like to continue testing, write down the secret phrase and restart the game, by refreshing your browser and select the 1st option this time. When playing in the normal mode, the Pathfind tool will be de-activated, and you will have to find the same items as before, this time using only your memory. Once you have collected 12 items, the key will again appear to show you the secret phrase. If the phrase matches the one, you wrote down then you have successfully completed the game. , and also opt to complete the second Google Form with questions tied to the game you played directly. Thank you.
Welcome, and thank you for taking the time to evaluate this game.
During practice mode you will be assisted in-game in order to find the items needed to recreate your recovery phrase. If you don't have a recovery phrase, you can use Mnemonic Code Converter to generate a unique phrase as if you had a Crypto Wallet. Select 12 words in the dropdown window, next to GENERATE button, then click the button to get a phrase in the "BIP39 Mnemonic" text area.
Once you select the 2nd option in the introduction screen of the game, you will be asked to input your recovery phrase. After doing so you can continue on by click the green button on the top-right corner. Please take the appropriate measures to secure your phrase, since you will have to use the same phrase for the next days.
When playing the game your task is to collect the 12 items in the order that is displayed in the Pathfind tool. You can interact with the Pathfind tool by pressing 'P' or using the button on the top-left corner.
You can click each button to get directions of where the item is and once you find it, you can collect it by pressing 'E'. Make sure to select the color of the item as displayed in the Pathfind tool. Once you have collected all 12 items, a small key button will appear to the bottom of the screen. Click that, and select Extract to find your recovery phrase!
If this is your first day of evaluating the game, then please make sure that you remember the location of all the items you collected, as well as the order, the color and the time of day you collected it. Everything is important in order to recreate your unique recovery phrase. It is advised to spent up to 2 hours playing in the practice mode using the same recovery phrase until you are somewhat comfortable with the route.
If this your second or third day of evaluation, then you probably already have a basic route in mind, so please take 10-15 minutes to do a run in practice mode and try not to use the Pathfind tool, unless you are stuck on an item.
On the third day of evaluation, after doing a practice run, restart the browser and this time select the 1st option in the introduction screen. Then head to the "Normal Mode" tab to finalize and fill out the second Form. You can opt to fill out the first Google Form, if you haven't done already.
If you are attempting normal mode, then you probably have followed the instructions correctly, and are on the third day of evaluation. Congratulations.
This time, you will have to collect the 12 items, using the route you have made over the past two days, and without the help of the Pathfind tool. When you collect all 12 items, the key button will appear, showing the recovery phrase you created. At this point you should have saved somewhere the correct recovery phrase, and are able to check if it matches.
In case you have the wrong phrase extracted, you can try to reorder the items in your inventory or possibly collect a different one, if you feel like you forgot something. This interaction is important in evaluating how this game can work as a secondary tool to help you memorize your route/phrase. If you failed to extract the correct phrase please make sure to report it in your Google Form response.
You can now finalize your evaluation of the game, by answering a few more questions in the second Google Form. Thank you.
